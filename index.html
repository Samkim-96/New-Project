About IYF

IYF Kenya was established as a worldwide youth organization and international NGO based on a Christian mindset in 2001. Their mission is to raise leaders with a global insight through youth education, activities, and provide realistic solutions to problems in the global village.

IYF Values

  • Change: A true change of life that begins from one's heart and then brings change to the world.
  • Challenge: A true challenge to overcome one's limitations and experience endless possibilities.
  • Cohesion: A true cohesive mind that transcends ethnicity, nationality, language, and religion.
